# Morse Chat 💬📡

Send dots and dashes to your friends using your micro:bit radio — just like a real Morse telegraph!

## How it works

| Action | What it does |
|--------|-------------|
| **Touch** the gold logo | Send a **dot** (short beep) |
| **Long press** the gold logo | Send a **dash** (long beep) |

When you **receive** from another micro:bit:
- A **dot** = one LED lights up in the centre, short tone
- A **dash** = three LEDs in a row, longer tone
- The screen clears after each symbol

> [!tip] Decode with your Morse card!
> Use the printed Morse alphabet card on your table to match dots and dashes to letters. You're the decoder!
