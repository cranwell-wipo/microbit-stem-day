---

## 🧊 What is a 3D printer?

A machine that **builds objects layer by layer** in 3 dimensions!

1. 🎨 You **design** an object on a computer
2. 🖨️ The printer **makes it for real**!
3. 🔥 It melts **plastic** (called "filament") and deposits it layer by layer
